<p><ul><li>Water-Based</li><li>Oil-Gel Based</li><li>Foam-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fracking fluids and chemicals can be categorized into three main types: Water-based, oil-gel based, and foam-based. Water-based fluids are the most commonly used type, consisting mainly of water and chemical additives. Oil-gel based fluids are created by mixing oil with gelling agents to increase viscosity. Foam-based fluids contain gas, surfactants, and water to produce a foam that can carry proppants into fractures. Each type of fracking fluid offers unique properties and benefits depending on the specific needs of the operation.</p></p>
