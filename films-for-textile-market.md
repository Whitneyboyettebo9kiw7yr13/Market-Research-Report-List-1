<p><ul><li>Breathable Films</li><li>Non-breathable Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Breathable films are typically used in textile applications where moisture management and breathability are key factors. These films allow air and moisture vapor to pass through while still providing protection against liquids and particles. Non-breathable films are used when impermeability is a priority, such as in medical or protective clothing. These films create a barrier against liquids and contaminants, without allowing air or moisture to pass through. Both types of films cater to different market needs based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
