<p><strong>The Simplified Voyage Data Recorder (S-VDR) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Float Free Capsule S-VDR</li><li>Fixed Capsule S-VDR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Simplified Voyage Data Recorder (S-VDR) market consists of two main types: Float Free Capsule S-VDR and Fixed Capsule S-VDR. Float Free Capsule S-VDR is designed to float free from the ship in case of sinking, allowing for easy recovery of crucial data. Fixed Capsule S-VDR, on the other hand, remains fixed in the ship and is commonly used on larger vessels. Both types of S-VDRs are essential in recording and storing critical data for use in accident investigations and improving maritime safety.</p></p>
