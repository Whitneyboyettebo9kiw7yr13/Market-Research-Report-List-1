<p><p>Type A Bare Metal Stents are characterized by their durability, strength, and high radial force. They are designed to provide long-term scaffolding to the blood vessel, preventing restenosis. Type B Bare Metal Stents, on the other hand, focus on flexibility and conformability, making them suitable for tortuous or bending vessels. Other types in the market include hybrid stents that combine the characteristics of Type A and Type B stents, offering a balance between strength, flexibility, and conformability to cater to different patient needs.</p></p>
