<p><p>Hair fixative polymer market consists of four main types based on their charge properties: anionic polymers, cationic polymers, non-ionic polymers, and amphoteric polymers. Anionic polymers carry a negative charge, while cationic polymers carry a positive charge. Non-ionic polymers do not carry any charge, and amphoteric polymers carry both positive and negative charges. Each type of polymer has specific characteristics that make them suitable for different hair fixative applications, making them important ingredients in various hair care products.</p></p>
