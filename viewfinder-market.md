<p><ul><li>Optical Viewfinder</li><li>Electronic Viewfinder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Viewfinder and Electronic Viewfinder are two main types of viewfinders in the camera market. Optical Viewfinders use lenses to provide a direct line of sight to the subject, offering a natural and lag-free viewing experience. On the other hand, Electronic Viewfinders use digital displays to show a real-time preview of the image being captured, allowing for additional information overlays and live adjustments. Both types cater to different preferences and shooting styles, with Optical Viewfinders favored for their clarity and simplicity, while Electronic Viewfinders offer more versatility and feature options.</p></p>
