<p><strong>The Conductive Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotropic Conductive Adhesive (ICA).</li><li>Anisotropic Conductive Adhesive (ACA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Conductive adhesives are divided into two main types: Isotropic Conductive Adhesive (ICA) and Anisotropic Conductive Adhesive (ACA). </p><p>ICA is a type of conductive adhesive that has uniformly distributed conductive particles, providing electrical conductivity in all directions. </p><p>On the other hand, ACA is a type of conductive adhesive that only allows electrical conductivity in one specified direction, making it ideal for applications requiring selective conductive paths. </p><p>Both types of conductive adhesives play critical roles in various industries where bonding and electrical conductivity are required.</p></p>
