<p><strong>The Commercial UPS Power Supply Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DC UPS</li><li>AC UPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial UPS power supply market types include DC UPS and AC UPS. DC UPS systems provide power from a battery source, ensuring uninterrupted electricity supply during outages. AC UPS systems use a rectifier to convert AC power to DC and then an inverter to convert it back to AC for devices. Both types offer reliable backup power solutions for various commercial applications, such as computers, servers, and critical machinery, to prevent data loss and downtime during power interruptions.</p></p>
