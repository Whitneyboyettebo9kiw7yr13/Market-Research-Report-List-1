<p><strong>The Solar Home Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grid-Tied Solar Home Lighting</li><li>Off-Grid Solar Home Lighting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Grid-Tied Solar Home Lighting systems are connected to the main grid, allowing them to draw power from the grid when needed and feed excess power back to the grid. This type of system is suitable for areas with reliable grid connections. On the other hand, Off-Grid Solar Home Lighting systems operate independently from the main grid, utilizing batteries to store excess power for use during periods of low sunlight. This option is ideal for remote or rural areas with limited access to the main grid.</p></p>
