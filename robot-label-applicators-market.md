<p><p>Robot label applicators come in two main market types: automatic modes and manual modes. Automatic mode applicators are designed to operate completely on their own, without the need for human intervention. These machines are programmed to apply labels at a set speed and accurately onto products. On the other hand, manual mode applicators require human guidance and control to apply labels. Users manually place the labels onto products, and the machine helps with the application process. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the labeling industry.</p></p>
