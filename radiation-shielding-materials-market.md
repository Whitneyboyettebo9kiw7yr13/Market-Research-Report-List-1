<p><p>Radiation shielding materials market can be categorized into three main types: Traditional Lead Shielding Materials, Lead Composite Shielding Materials, and Lead-Free Shielding Materials. Lead composite materials offer a lighter and more flexible alternative to pure lead. Lead-free shielding materials, such as tungsten and bismuth-based composites, are becoming more popular due to concerns about lead toxicity and environmental impact.
