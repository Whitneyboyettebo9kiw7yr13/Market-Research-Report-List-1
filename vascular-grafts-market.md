<p><p>Vascular grafts are medical devices used to replace or bypass damaged blood vessels. There are different types of vascular grafts available in the market, including polyester grafts, ePTFE (expanded polytetrafluoroethylene) grafts, polyurethane grafts, and biosynthetic grafts. Polyester grafts are durable and have good biocompatibility. ePTFE grafts are known for their high patency rates. Polyurethane grafts are flexible and can conform to the natural vessel shape. Biosynthetic grafts are made from a combination of synthetic and natural materials to promote better healing and integration within the body.</p></p>
