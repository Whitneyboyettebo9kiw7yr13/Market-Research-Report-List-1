<p><p>Packaging robots are classified into different types based on their functions and applications in the market. Picking robots are designed to pick items from a conveyor or storage area. Packing robots are used to carefully package products into boxes or containers. Case packing robots specifically handle the packing of products into larger cases or boxes. Tray packing robots focus on arranging products into trays for display or transportation. Filling robots are used to accurately fill containers with products such as liquids or powders. Each type of packaging robot plays a crucial role in optimizing the packaging process for different industries.</p></p>
