<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Power Steering</li><li>Electric Power Steering</li><li>Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heavy-duty truck steering systems come in three main types: Hydraulic Power Steering, Electric Power Steering, and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering. Hydraulic Power Steering uses a hydraulic fluid to assist in steering, Electric Power Steering uses an electric motor, and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering combines both hydraulic fluid and an electric motor for steering assistance. Each system has its own advantages and disadvantages, such as cost, efficiency, and maintenance requirements, catering to different preferences and requirements in the heavy-duty truck market.</p></p>
