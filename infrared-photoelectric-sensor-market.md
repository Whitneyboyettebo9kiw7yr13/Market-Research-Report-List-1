<p><p>Infrared photoelectric sensors are classified into two main types: active and passive. Active infrared sensors emit infrared light and detect any interruptions or reflections to trigger a response. They are commonly used in security systems and automatic doors. Passive infrared sensors, on the other hand, detect infrared radiation emitted by objects or individuals without emitting any light themselves. They are often used in motion detectors for lighting, alarms, and smart home automation systems. Both types play a crucial role in various applications due to their sensitivity and reliability.</p></p>
