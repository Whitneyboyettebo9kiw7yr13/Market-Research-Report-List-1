<p><p>Single-pane dive masks come in two main types: full and half. Full masks cover the entire face, providing maximum coverage and protection for the eyes, nose, and mouth. They are popular among divers who prefer a more secure fit and added comfort. Half masks, on the other hand, cover only the eyes and nose, leaving the mouth exposed. They are favored by divers who prioritize a wider field of vision and easier access to their mouth for communication or equipment adjustments.</p></p>
