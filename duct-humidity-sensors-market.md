<p><ul><li>Capacitive Sensor</li><li>Resistance Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Duct humidity sensors are commonly categorized into two types: capacitive sensors and resistance sensors. Capacitive sensors measure humidity by detecting changes in capacitance, while resistance sensors rely on changes in resistance to quantify humidity levels. Both types provide accurate and reliable readings, with capacitive sensors being more sensitive and faster in response, while resistance sensors are known for their cost-effectiveness. Each type has its own advantages and is used in different applications based on specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
