<p><p>Lighting for ICE & EVs market comprises three main types: Halogen Lighting, LED Lighting, and Xenon Lighting. Halogen lighting is a more traditional option, providing a yellowish light output and relatively lower energy efficiency. On the other hand, LED lighting is known for its bright and energy-efficient performance, making it a popular choice for modern vehicles. Xenon lighting falls between halogen and LED in terms of both brightness and energy efficiency, offering a high-intensity light output with a bluish tint.</p></p>
