<p><strong>The Automotive Active Suspension Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Suspension</li><li>Hydraulic Suspension</li><li>Electromagnetic Suspension</li><li>Electro-hydraulic Suspension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive active suspension market comprises various types, including air suspension, hydraulic suspension, electromagnetic suspension, and electro-hydraulic suspension. Air suspension systems use air springs to adjust ride height and stiffness. Hydraulic suspension systems use fluid-filled cylinders to provide damping and control. Electromagnetic suspension systems use electromagnets to adjust the damping effect. Electro-hydraulic suspension systems combine both electronic and hydraulic components to provide precise control over the vehicle's suspension. Each type offers unique features and benefits, catering to different preferences and requirements of vehicle owners.</p></p>
