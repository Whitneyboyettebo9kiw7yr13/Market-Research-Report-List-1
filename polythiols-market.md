<p><p>Polythiols are a type of compound with multiple thiol functional groups. General type polythiols are typically used in a wide range of applications such as adhesives, sealants, coatings, and elastomers. On the other hand, pre-catalyzed polythiols are specifically formulated with a catalyst included in the product, making them easier to use and reducing the need for additional processing steps. Both general type and pre-catalyzed polythiols offer unique properties and benefits, catering to different needs within the market.</p></p>
