<p><p>SBR Asphalt Modifier is available in two main types: SBR Latex and SBR Powder. SBR Powder, on the other hand, is a dry form of the modifier that is mixed into asphalt mixes to achieve similar benefits. Both types of SBR Asphalt Modifier enhance the performance and longevity of asphalt pavements, making them a popular choice in the construction industry.</p></p>
