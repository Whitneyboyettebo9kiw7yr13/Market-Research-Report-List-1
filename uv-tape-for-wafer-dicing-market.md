<p><p>UV tape for wafer dicing is used in the semiconductor industry during the dicing process to protect the wafer and keep the individual dies in place. There are two main types of UV tape on the market: single-sided and double-sided. Single-sided UV tape is coated with adhesive on only one side, while double-sided UV tape has adhesive on both sides. Each type offers specific benefits depending on the requirements of the dicing process, such as ease of handling and alignment precision.</p></p>
