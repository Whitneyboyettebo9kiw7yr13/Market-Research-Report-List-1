<p><p>Printed image sensors market types include inkjet printing, flexographic printing, screen printing, and others. Inkjet printing involves the precise deposition of ink droplets onto a substrate, allowing for high resolution and flexibility. Flexographic printing utilizes flexible relief plates to transfer ink onto a substrate, ideal for large-scale production. Screen printing involves passing ink through a mesh screen onto a substrate, offering durability and versatility. Other methods may include gravure printing, offset printing, or digital printing techniques.</p></p>
