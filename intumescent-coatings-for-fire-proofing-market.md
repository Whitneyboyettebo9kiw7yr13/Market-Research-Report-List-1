<p><p>Intumescent coatings for fireproofing come in two main market types: one-component coatings and multicomponent coatings. One-component coatings are easy to use and require no mixing before application, making them a convenient choice for many users. On the other hand, multicomponent coatings offer higher performance and durability, making them suitable for more demanding fireproofing applications. Both types of coatings are designed to expand and form a protective char when exposed to high temperatures, providing passive fire protection to the substrate they are applied to.</p></p>
