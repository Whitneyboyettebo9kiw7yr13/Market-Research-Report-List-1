<p><strong>The Cellphone Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IPS</li><li>ASV</li><li>TFT</li><li>OLED</li><li>STN</li><li>CSTN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cellphone display market is diverse, with several types of display technologies available. IPS (In-Plane Switching) offers high-quality color reproduction and wide viewing angles. ASV (Advanced Super View) provides improved contrast and color accuracy. TFT (Thin Film Transistor) displays are common for their fast response time and bright images. OLED (Organic Light-Emitting Diode) screens offer vibrant colors and energy efficiency. STN (Super Twisted Nematic) displays are cost-effective with good viewing angles. Finally, CSTN (Color Super Twisted Nematic) screens provide improved color accuracy compared to STN displays.</p></p>
