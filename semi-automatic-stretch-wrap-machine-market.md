<p><p>Semi-automatic stretch wrap machines come in three main types based on their wrapping method: Rotary Stretch Wrap Machine, Turntable Stretch Wrap Machine, and Robotic Stretch Wrap Machine. </p><p>Rotary stretch wrap machines have a rotating arm that wraps the film around the pallet. Turntable stretch wrap machines have a rotating turntable where the pallet spins while the film is applied. Robotic stretch wrap machines use robotic arms to apply the film. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for different packaging needs in various industries.</p></p>
