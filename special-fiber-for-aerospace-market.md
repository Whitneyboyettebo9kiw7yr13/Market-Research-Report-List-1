<p><p>Special fibers for aerospace applications come in two main types: conductive and insulation. Conductive fibers are designed to efficiently carry electrical current, making them ideal for applications such as electrostatic discharge protection and lightning strike protection. On the other hand, insulation fibers are engineered to provide thermal protection and prevent the transfer of heat, making them essential for maintaining temperature control in aircraft components. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and performance of aerospace systems.</p></p>
