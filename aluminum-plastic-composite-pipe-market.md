<p><strong>The Aluminum-plastic Composite Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PE/AL/PE</li><li>PE/AL/XPE</li><li>XPE/AL/XPE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminum-plastic composite pipes come in various types such as PE/AL/PE, PE/AL/XPE, and XPE/AL/XPE. PE/AL/PE pipes consist of a layer of polyethylene sandwiched between two layers of aluminum, offering high flexibility and corrosion resistance. PE/AL/XPE pipes feature a layer of cross-linked polyethylene in addition to the aluminum layers, providing better insulation and thermal performance. XPE/AL/XPE pipes have two layers of cross-linked polyethylene surrounding the aluminum core, offering enhanced durability and insulation properties.</p></p>
