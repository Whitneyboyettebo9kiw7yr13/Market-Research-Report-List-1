<p><ul><li>Mechanical Fasteners</li><li>Adhesive Fasteners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concrete fasteners can be classified into two main types: mechanical fasteners and adhesive fasteners. Mechanical fasteners include anchor bolts, screws, nails, and other types of hardware that are physically attached to the concrete surface. Adhesive fasteners, on the other hand, rely on adhesives such as epoxy to bond with the concrete and provide a strong connection without the need for drilling or hammering. Both types of fasteners have their own advantages and are used in various applications in the construction industry.</p></p>
