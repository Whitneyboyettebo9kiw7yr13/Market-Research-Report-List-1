<p><p>Immersion Liquid Cooling Technology offers two main types of cooling solutions: Two-phase Liquid Cooling and Single Phase Liquid Cooling. Two-phase Liquid Cooling involves immersing computer components in a dielectric fluid that boils and evaporates to dissipate heat efficiently. On the other hand, Single Phase Liquid Cooling utilizes a single fluid to remove heat from components without phase change. Both types of immersion liquid cooling technologies provide efficient cooling solutions for high-performance computing systems and data centers.</p></p>
