<p><p>Used and refurbished defibrillator market types include automatic external defibrillators, implantable cardioverter defibrillators, wearable cardioverter defibrillators, and advanced life support (ALS) units. Automatic external defibrillators are portable devices used for sudden cardiac arrest outside of a hospital setting. Implantable cardioverter defibrillators are surgically implanted devices that continuously monitor and regulate heart rhythms. Wearable cardioverter defibrillators are worn externally and monitor heart activity for at-risk patients. Advanced life support units are used by trained medical professionals to provide critical care in emergency situations.</p></p>
