<p><ul><li>Fresnel Screen</li><li>Black Grid Screen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical projection screens come in different types, including Fresnel screens and Black Grid screens. Fresnel screens have a grooved surface that helps in reflecting light uniformly, reducing hotspots and improving image quality. On the other hand, Black Grid screens have a grid structure embedded within the screen material to enhance contrast and improve visibility. Both types of screens cater to specific needs and preferences of users who require high-quality projection for various applications such as presentations, entertainment, and education.</p></p>
