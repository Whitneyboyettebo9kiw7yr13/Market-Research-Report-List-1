<p><p>Ultraviolet light sensors are divided into three main market types based on the wavelength of light they are designed to detect: UVA, UVB, and UVC. UVA sensors detect long-wavelength UV light, which can cause skin aging and damage. UVB sensors detect mid-wavelength UV light, which can cause sunburn and skin cancer. UVC sensors detect short-wavelength UV light, which is the most harmful and is typically blocked by the Earth's atmosphere. Each type of sensor is designed to provide specific protection and monitoring against different levels of UV exposure.</p></p>
