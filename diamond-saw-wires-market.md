<p><ul><li>Electroplated Wire</li><li>Resin Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diamond saw wires are essential tools used in cutting hard materials such as stone, concrete, and metal. There are two main types of diamond saw wires on the market: electroplated wire and resin wire. Electroplated wire is coated with diamond particles through an electroplating process, making it ideal for cutting through hard materials quickly. Resin wire, on the other hand, is made by embedding diamond particles in a resin bond, providing a smoother cutting experience and longer tool life. Both types cater to different cutting needs and preferences in the construction industry.</p></p>
