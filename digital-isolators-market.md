<p><strong>The Digital Isolators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Coupling</li><li>Magnetic Coupling</li><li>Giant Magnetoresistive GMR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital isolators are used to provide electrical isolation between two circuits while allowing data transmission. There are three main types of digital isolators based on the method of isolation: Capacitive Coupling, Magnetic Coupling, and Giant Magnetoresistive (GMR). Capacitive coupling isolators use capacitors to transmit digital signals across the isolation barrier. Magnetic coupling isolators use transformers to transmit signals. GMR isolators utilize changes in resistance to detect magnetic fields for signal transmission. Each type of isolator has its own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
