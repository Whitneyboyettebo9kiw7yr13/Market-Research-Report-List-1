<p><p>Plain bearings are used in various industries to provide smooth and efficient motion between two surfaces. There are four main types of plain bearings: journal, linear, thrust, and angular contact. Journal bearings are used for radial loads, linear bearings for linear motion, thrust bearings for axial loads, and angular contact bearings for combined radial and axial loads. Each type serves a specific purpose and is designed to handle a particular type of load, ensuring optimal performance and durability in diverse applications.</p></p>
