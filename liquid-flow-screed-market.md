<p><strong>The Liquid Flow Screed Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pumpable Calcium Sulphate Screed</li><li>Pumpable Cement Based Screed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid Flow Screed market has two main types: Pumpable Calcium Sulphate Screed and Pumpable Cement Based Screed. Pumpable Calcium Sulphate Screed is made from a blend of calcium sulphate binder, water, and aggregates. It provides a smooth finish and is suitable for underfloor heating systems. Pumpable Cement Based Screed, on the other hand, is made from a mixture of cement, sand, and water. It is known for its strength and durability, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications. Both types offer efficient and cost-effective solutions for floor leveling and construction projects.</p></p>
