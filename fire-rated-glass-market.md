<p><p>Fire rated glass can be classified into several types based on their composition and structure. Laminated fire resistant glass is made by bonding multiple layers of glass with a fire-resistant interlayer. Wired fire resistant glass has a wire mesh embedded within the glass to prevent shattering. Tempered fire resistant glass undergoes a heat treatment process to increase its strength. Ceramic fire resistant glass is made with a ceramic material that provides high fire resistance. Other types of fire rated glass include combinations of various materials to enhance fire protection properties.</p></p>
