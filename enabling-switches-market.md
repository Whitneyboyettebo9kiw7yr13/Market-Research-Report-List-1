<p><strong>The Enabling Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OFF-ON(2-position)</li><li>OFF-ON-OFF(3-position)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enabling switches come in two main types: OFF-ON, which is a 2-position switch that is either turned off or on, and OFF-ON-OFF, a 3-position switch that can be in the off position, on position, or off position again. These switches are commonly used in industrial machinery and equipment to provide a safety mechanism for operators. The OFF-ON switch allows for a simple on/off control, while the OFF-ON-OFF switch adds an extra level of safety by requiring the switch to be turned off before it can be turned on again.</p></p>
