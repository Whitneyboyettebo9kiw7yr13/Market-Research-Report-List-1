<p><p>Rooftop solar panels come in three main market types: monocrystalline silicon, polycrystalline silicon, and thin-film. Monocrystalline silicon panels are known for their high efficiency and sleek look, making them the most popular choice. Polycrystalline silicon panels are cost-effective and have a lower efficiency compared to monocrystalline. Thin-film panels are lightweight and flexible, making them easier to install on various surfaces. Each type of panel has its unique characteristics and suitability for different applications.</p></p>
