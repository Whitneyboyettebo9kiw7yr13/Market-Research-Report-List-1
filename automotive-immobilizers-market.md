<p><p>Automotive immobilizers are security devices used to prevent unauthorized access to vehicles. OEM immobilizers are installed by the original equipment manufacturer during production, providing seamless integration with the vehicle's existing security system. Aftermarket immobilizers are aftermarket products that can be installed on vehicles post-purchase. While OEM immobilizers offer higher levels of security and reliability, aftermarket immobilizers are more affordable and provide flexibility in customization and compatibility with a wider range of vehicles.</p></p>
