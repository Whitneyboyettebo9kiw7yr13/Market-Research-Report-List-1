<p><strong>The Speech Synthesis Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Concatenation Synthesis</li><li>Formant Synthesis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Speech synthesis software market types include concatenation synthesis and formant synthesis. </p><p>Concatenation synthesis uses a large database of recorded speech to create synthetic speech by combining multiple segments of recorded speech. This method allows for more natural-sounding and human-like speech output.</p><p>Formant synthesis, on the other hand, generates speech by modeling the human vocal tract and manipulating parameters such as pitch, volume, and duration. This method is often used for synthesizing artificial voices and can be adjusted to produce specific accents or vocal characteristics.</p></p>
