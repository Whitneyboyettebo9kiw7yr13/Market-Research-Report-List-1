<p><p>Pool Room Dehumidifiers come in two main types: Portable Dehumidifiers and Whole-home Dehumidifiers. Portable Dehumidifiers are smaller units that are easily moved from room to room and are ideal for smaller areas such as a single pool room. Whole-home Dehumidifiers are larger systems that are installed within the HVAC system of a home to remove excess moisture throughout the entire house, including the pool room. Both types of dehumidifiers help maintain a comfortable and healthy indoor environment by reducing humidity levels.</p></p>
