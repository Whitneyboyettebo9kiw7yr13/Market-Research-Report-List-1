<p><p>Hybrid Tunnel Boring Machines (TBMs) are versatile machines used in tunnel construction projects. Soft ground TBMs are designed for excavating tunnels in loose, sandy, or clay soil conditions, utilizing earth pressure balance technology. On the other hand, Hard Rock TBMs are suitable for tunneling through solid rock formations, utilizing disc cutters or rotary cutters to break through the rock. Both types of TBMs offer specialized solutions for different ground conditions, ensuring efficient and effective tunnel construction projects.</p></p>
