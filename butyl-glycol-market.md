<p><p>Butyl Glycol is available in two market types - E Series and P Series. E Series represents the ethylene glycol-based ethers, while the P Series consists of propylene glycol-based ethers. These two categories cater to different applications and industries depending on their specific properties and characteristics. E Series Butyl Glycol offers excellent solvent properties, while P Series Butyl Glycol is known for its lower volatility and higher boiling point, making it suitable for use in specialty coatings and other high-performance applications.</p></p>
