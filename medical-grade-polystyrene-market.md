<p><p>The medical grade Polystyrene market consists of three main types: General Purpose Polystyrene (GPPS), High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS), and Expanded Polystyrene (EPS). GPPS is a versatile and rigid plastic commonly used in medical devices such as test tubes and petri dishes. HIPS is a tougher form of polystyrene, often used in medical packaging and trays. EPS, known for its lightweight and insulating properties, is used in medical coolers and packaging for temperature-sensitive drugs and equipment. Each type offers unique characteristics to suit different medical industry needs.</p></p>
