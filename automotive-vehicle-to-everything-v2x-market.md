<p><ul><li>V2V</li><li>V2I</li><li>V2P</li></ul></p>
<p><p>V2X refers to vehicle-to-everything communication technology that enables vehicles to communicate with each other and with their surrounding infrastructure. V2V (vehicle-to-vehicle) allows vehicles to exchange information such as speed and position to improve safety and reduce accidents. V2I (vehicle-to-infrastructure) involves communication between vehicles and roadside infrastructure like traffic lights to enhance traffic flow and reduce congestion. V2P (vehicle-to-pedestrian) enables vehicles to detect and communicate with pedestrians to prevent accidents and improve pedestrian safety.</p></p>
