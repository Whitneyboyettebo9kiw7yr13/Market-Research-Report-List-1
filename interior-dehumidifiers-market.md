<p><strong>The Interior Dehumidifiers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Dehumidifier</li><li>Electronic Dehumidifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Interior dehumidifiers are categorized into two main types: mechanical dehumidifiers and electronic dehumidifiers. Mechanical dehumidifiers work by drawing in moist air, passing it over cold coils to condense the moisture, and then collecting the water in a tank. On the other hand, electronic dehumidifiers use a fan to pull in air, pass it over a refrigerated coil to condense the moisture, and then release the dry air back into the room. Both types are effective in reducing humidity levels indoors.</p></p>
