<p><ul><li>Honeycomb</li><li>GPF and DPF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive emissions ceramics market is divided into three main types: honeycomb, GPF (Gasoline Particulate Filter), and DPF (Diesel Particulate Filter). Honeycomb ceramics are used in catalytic converters to reduce harmful emissions, while GPFs are specifically designed to capture particulate matter from gasoline engines. DPFs, on the other hand, target diesel engines and trap soot and other particles. These ceramics play a crucial role in reducing pollution from vehicles and meeting stricter emission regulations around the world.</p></p>
