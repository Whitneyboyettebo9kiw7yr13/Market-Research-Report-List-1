<p><strong>The Gasoline EGR Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Pressure</li><li>High Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gasoline EGR valves are classified into two main types based on the pressure they operate at: low pressure and high pressure. Low pressure EGR valves are typically used in the intake manifold and work at lower pressure levels, while high pressure EGR valves are usually found in the exhaust system and operate at higher pressure levels. Both types of EGR valves serve the common purpose of recirculating exhaust gases back into the combustion chamber to reduce emissions and improve fuel efficiency in gasoline-powered vehicles.</p></p>
