<p><p>Road bollards are essential for traffic management and safety. The market for road bollards can be broadly categorized into two types: flexible bollards and rigid bollards. Flexible bollards are made of materials like rubber or plastic and are designed to bend upon impact, reducing damage to vehicles and bollards themselves. Rigid bollards, on the other hand, are made of durable materials like steel and are unable to flex upon impact. Each type offers advantages depending on the specific needs of a road or traffic management system.</p></p>
