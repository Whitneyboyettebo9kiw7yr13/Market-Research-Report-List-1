<p><strong>The Refractory PU Sandwich Board Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Metal Surface</li><li>Bimetallic Surface</li><li>Non-metallic Surface</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Refractory PU sandwich boards are available in three main market types based on their surface material: Single Metal Surface, Bimetallic Surface, and Non-metallic Surface. Single Metal Surface boards have a single metal coating which enhances heat resistance. Bimetallic Surface boards consist of two different metal layers, combining the properties of both to improve durability and thermal conductivity. Non-metallic Surface boards are coated with non-metal materials such as ceramics or polymers, offering unique advantages in terms of insulation and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
