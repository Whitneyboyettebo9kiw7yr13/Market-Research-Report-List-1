<p><strong>The Logic Comparator ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Collector</li><li>Totem-Pole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two popular market types of Logic Comparator ICs - Open Collector and Totem-Pole. Open Collector ICs have an output that can sink current but cannot source it, making them suitable for wired-OR configurations. Totem-Pole ICs, on the other hand, have both sinking and sourcing capabilities in their output, making them more versatile for a variety of applications. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages, so choosing the right one depends on the specific requirements of the circuit being designed.</p></p>
