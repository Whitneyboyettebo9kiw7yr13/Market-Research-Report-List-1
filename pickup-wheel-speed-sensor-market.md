<p><p>Pickup wheel speed sensors are widely used in automotive applications to monitor the speed of the wheels for various safety and performance functions. There are two main types of pickup wheel speed sensors in the market - Hall type sensors and magnetic electric type sensors. Hall type sensors use the Hall effect to measure the speed of the wheel, while magnetic electric type sensors work by generating an electric signal based on the rotation of a magnetic field. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring the efficient functioning of automotive systems.</p></p>
