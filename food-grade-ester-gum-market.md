<p><p>Food Grade Ester Gum market types include Polymerized Rosins of Glycerol Ester (PRGE), Glycerol Esters of Wood Rosin (GEWR), and Others. PRGE is derived from the polymerization of rosin with glycerol, providing excellent adhesion and film formation properties. GEWR is produced by esterifying wood rosin with glycerol, offering good solubility in oils and alcohol. The "Others" category encompasses various ester gums that may be derived from different raw materials or processed using alternative methods to suit specific food applications.</p></p>
