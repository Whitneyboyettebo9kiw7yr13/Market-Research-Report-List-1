<p><strong>The Aluminum Curtain Walls Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stick-built</li><li>Semi-unitized</li><li>Unitized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aluminum Curtain Walls market can be categorized into three types: Stick-built, Semi-unitized, and Unitized. Stick-built curtain walls involve the on-site assembly of individual components, providing flexibility but requiring more time and labor. Semi-unitized curtain walls are partially assembled off-site, reducing installation time but still requiring some on-site work. Unitized curtain walls are fully prefabricated off-site, allowing for speedy installation but limiting design flexibility. These variations offer different trade-offs in terms of customization, time, labor, and cost, allowing customers to choose the option that best suits their needs.</p></p>
