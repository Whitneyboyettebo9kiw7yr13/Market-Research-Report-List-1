<p><p>The Full-Frame CCD Image Sensors market can be divided into two types based on frame rate transmission. High frame rate transmission sensors are capable of capturing a large number of frames per second, making them suitable for applications requiring fast-moving objects to be captured with precision. On the other hand, low frame rate transmission sensors capture a lower number of frames per second, making them more suitable for applications where speed is not a critical factor. Both types of sensors cater to different market needs based on frame rate requirements.</p></p>
