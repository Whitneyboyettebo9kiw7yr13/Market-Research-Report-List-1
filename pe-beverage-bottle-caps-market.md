<p><p>PE beverage bottle caps come in two main market types: injection caps and compression mould caps. Injection caps are produced through the injection molding process, where molten plastic is injected into a mold and cooled to form the cap. Compression mould caps, on the other hand, are made by compressing plastic material in a mold under high pressure. Both types of caps offer different strengths and characteristics, catering to the diverse needs of the beverage industry.</p></p>
