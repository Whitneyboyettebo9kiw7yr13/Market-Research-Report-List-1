<p><p>Ultra High Gloss Acrylic Sheets are available in two main types: Extruded Acrylic Sheet and Cast Acrylic Sheet. Extruded Acrylic Sheet is produced using a continuous manufacturing process, resulting in a more affordable option with consistent thickness. Cast Acrylic Sheet, on the other hand, is made by pouring liquid acrylic into molds and allowing it to harden, providing higher quality and better optical clarity. Both types are highly durable, scratch-resistant, and offer a sleek, glossy finish, making them popular choices for various applications.</p></p>
