<p><strong>The Automotive OEM Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Borne</li><li>Waterborne</li><li>Powdered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive OEM coatings are available in three main types: solvent-borne, waterborne, and powdered coatings. Solvent-borne coatings use organic solvents to disperse the resin and additives, providing a durable and high-quality finish. Waterborne coatings use water as a carrier for the resin and additives, offering environmental advantages and lower VOC emissions. Powdered coatings are applied as a dry powder and then melted and cured to form a protective film, providing excellent corrosion resistance and durability. Each type offers unique benefits to meet different application needs in the automotive industry.</p></p>
