<p><strong>The Suspended-Level Viscometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Suspended-Level Viscometers are classified into Type I and Type II markets based on their design and application. Type I viscometers are traditional instruments that measure viscosity by observing the displacement of a weighted float in a liquid. They are simple and provide accurate results for a wide range of fluids. On the other hand, Type II viscometers employ advanced technology such as laser sensors or digital displays to measure viscosity with higher precision and accuracy. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the viscometer market.</p></p>
