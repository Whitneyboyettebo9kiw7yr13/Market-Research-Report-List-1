<p><p>Three phase bridge rectifiers are available in three different market types: SMD/SMT, Screw Mount, and Through Hole. SMD/SMT bridge rectifiers are surface mount devices, suitable for automated assembly processes and compact designs. Screw Mount bridge rectifiers are designed for applications where secure mounting is necessary, providing stability and durability. Through Hole bridge rectifiers have leads that are inserted into holes on the circuit board for secure attachment, making them ideal for applications where reliability and ease of maintenance are key.</p></p>
