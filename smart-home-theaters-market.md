<p><ul><li>HTIB(Home Theater in a Box)</li><li>Component Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart Home Theaters come in two main types, HTIB (Home Theater in a Box) and Component Systems. HTIBs are all-in-one systems that include everything needed for a home theater experience in a single package. They are convenient and easy to set up. Component Systems, on the other hand, allow for more customization and flexibility as users can choose individual components like receivers, speakers, and Blu-ray players. While Component Systems offer better performance and quality, HTIBs are more budget-friendly and user-friendly for those looking for a simpler setup.</p></p>
