<p><strong>The Flexi-bag Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monolayer</li><li>Multilayer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexi-bag market consists of two main types: Monolayer and Multilayer. Monolayer flexi-bags are made of a single layer of material and are commonly used for products that do not require high barrier properties. On the other hand, Multilayer flexi-bags are made of multiple layers of materials, providing better barrier properties and durability. Multilayer flexi-bags are typically used for products sensitive to oxygen or moisture, such as food or pharmaceuticals. Both types cater to different market needs based on the level of protection required for the product.</p></p>
