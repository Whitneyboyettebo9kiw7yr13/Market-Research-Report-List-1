<p><ul><li>Parallel Hybrid</li><li>Series Hybrid</li><li>Series-parallel Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hybrid powertrain systems combine an internal combustion engine with an electric motor to improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions. There are three main types of hybrid powertrain systems on the market: Parallel Hybrid, where both the engine and electric motor can drive the wheels independently or together; Series Hybrid, where only the electric motor drives the wheels while the engine generates power for the electric motor; and Series-Parallel Hybrid, a combination of both parallel and series designs for more flexibility and efficiency.</p></p>
