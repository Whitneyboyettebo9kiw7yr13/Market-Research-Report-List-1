<p><p>Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Systems are segmented into two main types: 2D AOI Systems and 3D AOI Systems. 2D AOI Systems use cameras to capture images of the PCB surface to detect defects such as missing components or misalignment. On the other hand, 3D AOI Systems utilize advanced imaging technology to not only detect surface defects but also inspect solder joints and measure dimensions accurately. Both systems play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of electronics manufacturing processes.</p></p>
