<p><strong>The Bench Top Automatic Cell Sorter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Cell Sorter</li><li>Fluorescent Activated Cell Sorter</li><li>Magnetic Cell Sorter</li><li>Buoyancy Activated Cell Sorter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bench top automatic cell sorters are classified into different types based on their mechanisms of sorting cells. The single cell sorter is capable of isolating individual cells from a heterogeneous population. Fluorescent activated cell sorters use fluorescent markers to identify and sort cells based on their characteristics. Magnetic cell sorters separate cells using magnetic beads attached to specific cell markers. Buoyancy activated cell sorters sort cells based on their density. Each type of cell sorter offers unique capabilities for researchers and clinicians in various fields of study.</p></p>
