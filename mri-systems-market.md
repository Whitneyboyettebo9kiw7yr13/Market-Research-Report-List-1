<p><p>MRI Systems are divided into two main types based on their design: Open MRI systems and Closed MRI systems. Open MRI systems are more spacious and provide a more comfortable experience for patients, especially those who may feel claustrophobic. Closed MRI systems, on the other hand, offer higher image quality due to their enclosed design, which reduces interference from outside sources. Both types have their advantages and are used in different medical facilities based on patient needs and imaging requirements.</p></p>
