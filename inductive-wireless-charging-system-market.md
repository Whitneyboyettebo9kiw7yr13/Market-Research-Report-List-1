<p><p>Inductive Wireless Charging System is divided into two main market types: Electromagnetic Induction and Magnetic Resonance. </p><p>Electromagnetic Induction technology uses a magnetic field to transfer energy between two coils, one in the charging pad and the other in the device being charged. This method is widely used in products like smartphones and wearables.</p><p>On the other hand, Magnetic Resonance technology allows for greater distance and positioning flexibility between the charging pad and the device. This makes it ideal for applications in furniture, automotive, and medical devices.</p></p>
