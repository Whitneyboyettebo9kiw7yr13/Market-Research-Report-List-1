<p><p>Multi-axis industrial robots are categorized based on the number of axes they have, with each type offering different levels of flexibility and capability. Two-axis robots offer movement in two planes, three-axis robots in three planes, four-joint SCARA robots provide advanced horizontal and vertical movements, five-axis robots enable increased range of motion, six-axis robots offer the highest level of flexibility and precision. There are also other variations like gantry robots, Delta robots, and articulated robots that cater to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
