<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Type I FRP panels and sheets are typically made of a single type of fiber reinforcement, such as carbon or glass, providing high strength but with limited flexibility. These are commonly used in applications where structural stability is a key factor, such as in construction or aerospace.</p><p>On the other hand, Type II FRP panels and sheets consist of a combination of different fiber reinforcements, offering a balance between strength and flexibility. These are often used in applications where a more versatile material is required, such as in automotive or marine industries.</p></p>
