<p><p>Proximity sensors market is classified into different types based on their working principles such as inductive, capacitive, LVDT, ultrasonic, and photoelectric sensors. Inductive sensors use electromagnetic fields to detect metallic objects. Capacitive sensors sense the change in capacitance when an object enters their detection range. LVDT sensors measure linear displacement with high accuracy. Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to detect objects. Photoelectric sensors use light beams to detect the presence of objects. Each type of sensor has specific applications and advantages in various industries.</p></p>
