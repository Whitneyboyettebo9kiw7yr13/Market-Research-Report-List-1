<p><strong>The Standalone Medical Vacuum System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Claw Pump Techology</li><li>Dry Rotary Vane Technology</li><li>Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The standalone medical vacuum system market includes three main types of technologies: dry claw pump technology, dry rotary vane technology, and oil sealed rotary vane technology. </p><p>Dry claw pump technology uses two multi-claw rotors that do not come into contact with each other, making it highly reliable and low maintenance. </p><p>Dry rotary vane technology utilizes rotating vanes to create vacuum pressure without the need for oil, ideal for medical applications where contamination is a concern. </p><p>Oil sealed rotary vane technology uses oil as a lubricant to create vacuum pressure, offering high efficiency and durability.</p></p>
