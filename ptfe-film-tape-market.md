Low Density tapes are lightweight and flexible, suitable for applications requiring minimal thickness. Standard Density tapes offer a balance of strength and flexibility. Medium Density tapes provide enhanced strength and durability for more demanding applications. High Density tapes are the thickest and strongest, offering maximum durability and resistance to wear and tear. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
