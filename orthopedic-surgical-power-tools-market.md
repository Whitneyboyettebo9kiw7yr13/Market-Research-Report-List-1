<p><ul><li>Electric Powered</li><li>Battery Operated</li><li>Pneumatic Powered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The orthopedic surgical power tools market consists of three main types of tools: electric powered, battery operated, and pneumatic powered. Electric powered tools utilize electricity as their energy source and are commonly used in surgical procedures due to their high power and reliability. Battery operated tools, as the name suggests, are powered by a rechargeable battery and offer the advantage of portability and freedom of movement. Pneumatic powered tools utilize compressed air as their source of power and are often used in situations where electrical power is not readily available or safe to use.</p></p>
