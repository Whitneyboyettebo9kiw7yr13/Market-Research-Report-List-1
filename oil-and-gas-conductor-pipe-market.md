ERW pipes are manufactured by welding longitudinally, while SSAW pipes are made by welding spirally. LSAW pipes involve welding longitudinally and are suitable for large diameter pipes. Other types may include seamless pipes or seamless-ERW hybrid pipes, each with their own advantages and applications in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
