<p><p>Condenser water systems are used in HVAC systems to remove heat from the system by circulating water through the condenser coils. There are two main types of condenser water systems: direct cooling water systems and circulating cooling water systems. </p><p>Direct cooling water systems directly cool the refrigerant by passing water through the condenser coils, while circulating cooling water systems use a closed-loop system to circulate water through a cooling tower to cool the water before it is circulated through the condenser coils.</p></p>
