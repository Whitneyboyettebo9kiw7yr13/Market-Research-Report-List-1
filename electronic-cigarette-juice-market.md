<p><p>The electronic cigarette juice market consists of three main types: PG base, VG base, and a blend of both. PG base refers to e-liquids that primarily use propylene glycol, a synthetic liquid that provides a stronger throat hit and carries flavor well. VG base, on the other hand, uses vegetable glycerin, which produces denser vapor but may mute flavors slightly. Blend PG & VG market combines both propylene glycol and vegetable glycerin, offering a balance between throat hit and vapor production while enhancing the taste of the vape.</p></p>
