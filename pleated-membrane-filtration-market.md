<p><p>Pleated membrane filtration market includes various types such as PP filter, PES filter, PTFE filter, Nylon filter, and others. PP filters are known for their high dirt holding capacity and excellent chemical resistance. PES filters offer superior flow rates and low protein binding. PTFE filters are chemically inert and have high temperature resistance. Nylon filters provide high mechanical strength and compatibility with a wide range of solvents. Other filters in the market offer specific advantages based on their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
