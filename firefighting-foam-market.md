<p><p>Firefighting foam is categorized into three main types: Protein Foam, Synthetic Foam, and Class A foam. Protein foam is made from natural animal proteins and is commonly used for extinguishing flammable liquid fires. Synthetic foam is made from synthetic chemicals and is more versatile, suitable for use on various types of fires. Class A foam is specifically designed for combating Class A fires, such as wood, paper, and fabric fires, by lowering the surface tension of water and enhancing its ability to penetrate materials.</p></p>
