<p><ul><li>Lead Acid Battery</li><li>Lithium-Based Battery</li><li>Nickel-Based Battery</li><li>Other Batteries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The battery recycling market comprises various types, including lead-acid batteries, lithium-based batteries, nickel-based batteries, and other batteries. Lead-acid batteries are commonly found in vehicles and require recycling due to their lead content. Lithium-based batteries are widely used in portable electronics and electric vehicles and need recycling to recover valuable metals like lithium and cobalt. Nickel-based batteries, like nickel-cadmium and nickel-metal hydride, are found in power tools and need recycling to prevent environmental pollution. Lastly, the category of other batteries includes various types such as alkaline, zinc-carbon, and button cells, which require recycling to minimize waste and environmental impact.</p></p>
