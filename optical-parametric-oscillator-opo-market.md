<p><ul><li>Single Resonance</li><li>Double Resonance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Parametric Oscillator (OPO) market can be classified into Single Resonance and Double Resonance types. In Single Resonance OPO, the pump radiation interacts with the signal and idler waves in a single nonlinear crystal, resulting in a simple design and cost-effective solution for specific applications. On the other hand, Double Resonance OPO utilizes two nonlinear crystals and resonant cavities for signal and idler waves, offering enhanced tunability and efficiency for more demanding applications. Both types cater to different market segments based on their unique characteristics and performance capabilities.</p></p>
