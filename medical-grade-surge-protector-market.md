<p><strong>The Medical Grade Surge Protector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voltage Switch Type</li><li>Pressure Limiting Type</li><li>Combination Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical grade surge protectors come in three main types based on their protection mechanisms:</p><p>1. Voltage Switch Type: These surge protectors detect overvoltage and automatically switch to a safe level to protect connected equipment.</p><p>2. Pressure Limiting Type: This type limits the amount of electrical pressure reaching the equipment during a surge, preventing damage.</p><p>3. Combination Type: These surge protectors combine the features of voltage switch and pressure limiting types to provide comprehensive protection against power surges in medical settings.</p></p>
