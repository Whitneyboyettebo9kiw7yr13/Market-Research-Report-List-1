<p><strong>The Thermoforming Plastic Packing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Formed</li><li>Pressure Formed</li><li>Mechanical Formed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermoforming plastic packaging market includes three main types: vacuum formed, pressure formed, and mechanical formed packaging. Vacuum formed packaging is created by heating a plastic sheet and using vacuum pressure to form it into a mold. Pressure formed packaging involves applying pressure to the heated plastic sheet to shape it into a mold. Mechanical formed packaging uses mechanical force to shape the plastic sheet into a mold. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in various industries for packaging purposes.</p></p>
