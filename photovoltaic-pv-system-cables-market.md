<p><p>Photovoltaic (PV) system cables are categorized into two main types: DC cables and AC cables. DC cables are used to connect the solar panels to the inverter, carrying the direct current generated by the panels. AC cables, on the other hand, are used to connect the inverter to the electrical grid, carrying the alternating current converted by the inverter. Both types of cables are crucial components of a PV system, ensuring the efficient and safe transmission of electricity.</p></p>
