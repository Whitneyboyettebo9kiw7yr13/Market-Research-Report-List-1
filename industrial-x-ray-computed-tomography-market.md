<p><strong>The Industrial X-ray Computed Tomography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Line Beam Scanning</li><li>In Cone Beam Scanning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial X-ray Computed Tomography is a non-destructive testing method used for analyzing the internal structures of objects. In Line Beam Scanning, a narrow X-ray beam is used to scan the object line by line, producing high-resolution images suitable for detailed analysis. In Cone Beam Scanning, a cone-shaped X-ray beam is used to capture a 3D image of the entire object at once, making it faster but slightly lower in resolution compared to Line Beam Scanning. Both methods have their own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
