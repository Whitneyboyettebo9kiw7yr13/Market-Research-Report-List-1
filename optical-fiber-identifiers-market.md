<p><p>Optical Fiber Identifiers (OFI) are crucial tools for maintenance and troubleshooting of fiber optic networks. There are two main types of Optical Fiber Identifiers in the market - OFI-BIPM and OFI-BI. OFI-BIPM identifies the presence of an optical signal without disrupting the transmission, while OFI-BI can additionally detect the direction of the signal flow. Both types play a significant role in identifying and verifying fiber optic signals in various network applications.</p></p>
