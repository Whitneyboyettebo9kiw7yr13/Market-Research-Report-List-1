<p><p>Thermally-Modified Timber (TMT) is a popular wood treatment process that enhances the durability and stability of timber, making it more suitable for outdoor use. There are two main market types for TMT: Thermo-S and Thermo-D. Thermo-S TMT involves heating the wood in a steam environment, while Thermo-D TMT involves heating the wood in an oxygen-deficient environment. Both types of TMT result in timber that is resistant to rot, decay, and insect damage, making them ideal for a variety of outdoor applications.</p></p>
