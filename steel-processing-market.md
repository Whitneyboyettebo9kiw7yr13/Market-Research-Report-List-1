<p><p>Steel processing market can be broadly categorized into two types: carbon steel and alloy steel. Carbon steel is primarily made up of iron and carbon, with small amounts of other elements. It is widely used in construction, automotive, and manufacturing industries due to its strength and affordability. On the other hand, alloy steel contains additional elements like chromium, nickel, and molybdenum, which enhance its properties such as strength, corrosion resistance, and heat resistance. Alloy steel is commonly used in aerospace, automotive, and oil & gas industries.</p></p>
