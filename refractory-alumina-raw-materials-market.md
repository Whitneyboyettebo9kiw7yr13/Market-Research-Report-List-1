<p><strong>The Refractory Alumina Raw Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Calcined Alumina</li><li>Fused Alumina</li><li>Tabular Alumina</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Refractory alumina raw materials market consists of various types such as Calcined Alumina, Fused Alumina, Tabular Alumina, and others. Calcined Alumina is produced by heating alumina hydrate to high temperatures, resulting in a fine white powder used in refractory products. Fused Alumina is made by melting alumina in electric arc furnaces and is known for its high hardness and strength. Tabular Alumina is produced by sintering alumina at high temperatures, creating a dense, hard material suited for refractory applications. Others include speciality alumina products catering to specific industry needs.</p></p>
