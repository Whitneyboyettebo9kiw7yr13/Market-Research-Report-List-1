<p><p>Rollerball pens are available in two main types on the market: capped and retractable. Capped rollerball pens require the user to remove a cap in order to access the pen tip, providing a secure seal when not in use. Retractable rollerball pens, on the other hand, have a mechanism that allows the pen tip to be extended and retracted with a simple click or twist, eliminating the need for a cap. Both types offer convenience and different styles to suit individual preferences.</p></p>
