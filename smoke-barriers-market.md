<p><strong>The Smoke Barriers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ActiveType</li><li>PassiveType</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active-type smoke barriers use mechanical systems such as smoke exhaust fans and dampers to actively control the movement of smoke in a building during a fire. Passive-type smoke barriers, on the other hand, rely on physical barriers such as fire-rated walls, doors, and smoke curtains to contain smoke and prevent its spread. Both types are essential in ensuring the safety of occupants and limiting property damage in the event of a fire. The market for these smoke barriers is driven by building regulations and the increasing focus on fire safety in commercial and residential structures.</p></p>
