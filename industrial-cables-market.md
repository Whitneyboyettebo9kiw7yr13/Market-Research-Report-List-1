<p><p>Industrial cables market can be categorized into three main types based on voltage requirements: Low Voltage, Medium Voltage, and High Voltage. Low voltage cables are used for applications that require voltages below 1kV, such as small machinery or appliances. Medium voltage cables are designed for voltages between 1kV to 35kV and are commonly used in industrial settings like power distribution networks. High voltage cables are meant for voltages above 35kV and are utilized in high-power applications like substations and large industrial machinery.</p></p>
