<p><strong>The Electrode Spark Plug Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Sparkplug</li><li>Cold Sparkplug</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrode spark plug market is categorized into two main types: hot spark plugs and cold spark plugs. Hot spark plugs are designed to maintain a higher temperature at the electrode tip, which prevents fouling in engines that run at lower temperatures. Cold spark plugs, on the other hand, are designed to dissipate heat more effectively, making them ideal for engines that operate at higher temperatures. The choice between hot and cold spark plugs depends on the specific requirements of the engine and its operating conditions.</p></p>
