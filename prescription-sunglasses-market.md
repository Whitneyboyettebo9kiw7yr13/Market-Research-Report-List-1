<p><p>Prescription sunglasses market offers several options for lenses, including glass, CR-39, polycarbonate, polyurethane, and other materials. Glass lenses provide excellent optical clarity but are heavier and more prone to breakage. CR-39 lenses are lightweight and offer good optical quality but are not as impact-resistant as polycarbonate lenses. Polycarbonate lenses are highly impact-resistant and often used in sports sunglasses. Polyurethane lenses are lightweight and flexible, making them comfortable to wear. Other materials may include high-index plastics or photochromic lenses for added functionality.</p></p>
