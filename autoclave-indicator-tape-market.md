<p><ul><li>Steam Indicator Tapes</li><li>Hydrogen Peroxide Tapes</li><li>EO Sterilization Indicator Tapes</li><li>Dry Heat Tapes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Autoclave indicator tapes are used to monitor sterilization processes in healthcare facilities. Steam indicator tapes change color when exposed to steam, indicating that the sterilization process has occurred. Hydrogen peroxide tapes change color in the presence of hydrogen peroxide, used in low-temperature sterilization methods. EO sterilization indicator tapes change color when exposed to ethylene oxide gas. Dry heat tapes change color when exposed to high temperatures in dry heat sterilization processes. Each type of indicator tape is used to ensure that medical instruments and equipment are properly sterilized.</p></p>
