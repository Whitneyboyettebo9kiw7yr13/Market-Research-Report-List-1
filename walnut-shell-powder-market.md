<p><p>Walnut shell powder, also known as walnut grit or walnut media, is a natural abrasive made from crushed walnut shells. The powder is obtained after the shells undergo a grinding and sieving process. Walnut shell powder is widely used in various industries such as cosmetics, cleaning products, oil drilling, and polishing.</p><p>The market for walnut shell powder is witnessing significant growth due to its eco-friendly and biodegradable properties. With the increasing consumer awareness about the harmful effects of chemical-based products, the demand for natural and organic alternatives is rising. Walnut shell powder fits this requirement perfectly, as it is a natural, non-toxic, and sustainable substitute for synthetic abrasives.</p><p>One of the key drivers of the walnut shell powder market is its extensive usage in the cosmetics industry. Walnut shell powder is commonly used as an exfoliating agent in facial scrubs, body washes, and soaps. The growing trend of personal grooming and skincare has resulted in increased demand for exfoliating products, thereby driving the market growth.</p><p>In addition to cosmetics, walnut shell powder is also widely used in the cleaning industry. It is an effective abrasive for cleaning surfaces like metals, plastics, and wood. The powder's ability to remove tough stains and residues has led to its adoption in household cleaning products and industrial cleaners.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing a surge in demand from the oil drilling industry. Walnut shell powder is used as a proppant in hydraulic fracturing, where it helps to prop open fractures in the rock formation, allowing the extraction of oil and gas. The expanding oil and gas exploration activities globally are driving the market growth in this sector.</p><p>Overall, with the increasing demand for natural and sustainable products, along with its diverse applications in various industries, the walnut shell powder market is projected to witness significant growth.
