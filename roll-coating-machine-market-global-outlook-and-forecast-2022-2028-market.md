<p><p>The roll coating machine market refers to the industry that produces and sells machines used for coating various materials. The market can be categorized into different types of coatings, including open coating, closed coating, post-metering coating, blade roll coating, and other coatings. Open coating refers to a process where the coating is applied only on one side of the material. Closed coating involves applying the coating on both sides of the material. Post-metering coating is a method where the coating amount is controlled after the material is metered. Blade roll coating utilizes a blade to coat the material, and other coatings include various techniques not classified under the aforementioned types.
