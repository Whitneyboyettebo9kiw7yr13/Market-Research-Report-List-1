<p><p>Transmission speed sensors are categorized into two types based on their functions: input speed sensors and output speed sensors. Input speed sensors monitor the rotational speed of the input shaft of the transmission, providing crucial data for accurate gear selection and smooth shifting. Output speed sensors, on the other hand, measure the rotational speed of the output shaft, facilitating control of vehicle speed and engine performance. Both types of sensors play a vital role in ensuring the efficiency and reliability of the transmission system.</p></p>
