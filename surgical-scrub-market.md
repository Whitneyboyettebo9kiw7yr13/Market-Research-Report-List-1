<p><p>The Surgical Scrub market can be categorized into two types: Waterless and Water-based. Waterless surgical scrubs are convenient and quick to use as they do not require rinsing with water. They are preferred in situations where water availability is limited or for quick touch-ups. They are commonly used in healthcare settings where thorough disinfection is necessary for surgical procedures.</p></p>
