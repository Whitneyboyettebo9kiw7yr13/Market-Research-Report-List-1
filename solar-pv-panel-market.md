<p><p>Solar PV panels are available in three main market types: monocrystalline, polycrystalline, and thin-film. Monocrystalline panels are known for their high efficiency and sleek appearance, making them ideal for residential and commercial use. Polycrystalline panels are more affordable and have a slightly lower efficiency compared to monocrystalline panels, making them a popular choice for budget-conscious consumers. Thin-film panels are lightweight, flexible, and easy to install, making them ideal for applications where traditional panels may not be suitable.</p></p>
