<p><p>On-Board Magnetic Hall Effect Sensors are classified into three main types based on their sensitivity to magnetic fields. Low-Field Sensors are designed to detect very weak magnetic fields below 1 Microgauss. Earth Field Sensors can detect magnetic fields ranging from 1 Microgauss to 10 Gauss, which includes the Earth's magnetic field. Bias Magnetic Field Sensors are capable of detecting magnetic fields above 10 Gauss, making them suitable for applications requiring sensing of strong magnetic fields. Each type of sensor is optimized for different magnetic field strengths.</p></p>
