<p><strong>The Non-insulated Fire Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monolithic Non-insulated Fire Glass</li><li>Composite Non-insulated Fire Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Monolithic Non-insulated Fire Glass refers to fire-resistant glass made from a single piece of material, offering basic protection against flames and heat. On the other hand, Composite Non-insulated Fire Glass is made by combining multiple layers of glass with interlayers to enhance fire resistance and safety. Monolithic Non-insulated Fire Glass is more budget-friendly and suitable for basic fire protection needs, while Composite Non-insulated Fire Glass provides higher levels of protection and is often used in high-risk environments.</p></p>
