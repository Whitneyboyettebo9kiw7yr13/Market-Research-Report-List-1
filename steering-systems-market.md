<p><strong>The Steering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic Power Steering (EPS)</li><li>Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS)</li><li>Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Steering Systems market comprises of three main types: Electronic Power Steering (EPS), Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS), and Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS). EPS uses an electric motor to assist with steering, EHPS combines electric and hydraulic systems, while HPS relies solely on hydraulic fluid to aid steering. EPS is gaining popularity due to its efficiency and ease of integration with advanced driver assistance systems. EHPS offers a balance between EPS and traditional HPS, while HPS remains a reliable and cost-effective option for many vehicles.</p></p>
