<p><ul><li>Open Loop</li><li>Closed Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive current sensor market is divided into two types: open loop and closed loop. Open loop sensors measure current without feedback and are generally less accurate but more cost-effective. Closed loop sensors, on the other hand, provide feedback mechanisms to improve accuracy and reliability in measuring current. They are typically used in more critical applications where precision is crucial. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the automotive system.</p></p>
