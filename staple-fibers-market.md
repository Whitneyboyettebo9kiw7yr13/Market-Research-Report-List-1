Cellulosic fibers are also natural fibers derived from plant sources such as wood pulp and are known for their absorbency and comfort. Polyester fibers are synthetic fibers made from petroleum-based chemicals and are valued for their durability and resistance to wrinkling. These staple fibers play a crucial role in various industries and applications.</p></p>
