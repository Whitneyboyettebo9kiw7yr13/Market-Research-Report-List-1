<p><p>Process gas screw compressors are used in various industries for gas compression applications. There are two main types of process gas screw compressors: oil-free screw compressors and oil-injected screw compressors. Oil-free screw compressors are designed to provide clean and uncontaminated compressed air, making them suitable for applications where oil carryover is a concern. On the other hand, oil-injected screw compressors utilize oil for lubrication and sealing purposes, making them more cost-effective and efficient for certain applications.</p></p>
