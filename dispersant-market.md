<p><p>Dispersants can be categorized into different market types based on their physical form. Suspensions are formulations where solid particles are dispersed in a liquid. Colloids involve particles dispersed in a medium, forming a stable mixture. Solutions are homogeneous mixtures where the dispersant is completely dissolved in a solvent. Lastly, powder dispersants are solid particles that can be easily dispersed in liquids. Each of these market types offers unique properties and benefits for various applications in industries such as chemicals, agriculture, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
