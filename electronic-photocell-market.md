<p><strong>The Electronic Photocell Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Center Cathode Photocell</li><li>Center Anode Photocell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic photocells are categorized into two main types: Center Cathode Photocell and Center Anode Photocell. In a Center Cathode Photocell, the cathode is placed at the center of the photocell, emitting electrons towards the anode when light is detected. On the other hand, in a Center Anode Photocell, the anode is placed at the center, attracting electrons from the cathode when light is present. These two types of photocells have specific applications and advantages in various electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
