<p><p>Medical Lithotripsy Device Market consists of two main types- Intracorporeal Lithotripsy and Extracorporeal Lithotripsy. Intracorporeal Lithotripsy involves the use of a device inserted directly into the body to break down kidney stones. On the other hand, Extracorporeal Lithotripsy involves the use of shock waves from outside the body to break down stones located in the kidneys or other parts of the urinary tract. Both types offer minimally invasive treatment options for patients suffering from kidney stones.</p></p>
