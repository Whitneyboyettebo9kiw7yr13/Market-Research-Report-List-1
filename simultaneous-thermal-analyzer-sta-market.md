<p><p>A simultaneous thermal analyzer (STA) is a type of analytical instrument that combines the capabilities of a thermogravimetric analyzer (TGA) and a differential scanning calorimeter (DSC) in a single device. This enables simultaneous measurement of both the weight change and heat flow of a sample as it is subjected to controlled temperature changes. </p><p>The STA market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years, with a projected CAGR of 13.7% during the forecast period.
