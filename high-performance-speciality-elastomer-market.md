<p><p>High Performance Speciality Elastomer market consists of two main types: Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE) and Thermosetting Elastomers (TSE). TPEs are elastic materials that can be repeatedly softened and hardened through heating and cooling, making them versatile and easy to process. TSEs, on the other hand, undergo a chemical reaction during curing that permanently sets their shape and properties, making them durable and heat-resistant. Both types of elastomers offer unique characteristics and are utilized in various industries for their high performance capabilities.</p></p>
