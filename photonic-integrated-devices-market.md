<p><p>Photonic integrated devices market includes various types of components such as lasers, modulators, detectors, attenuators, multiplexers/de-multiplexers, and optical amplifiers. Lasers are used for emitting coherent light, modulators for manipulating signals, detectors for capturing light intensity, attenuators for reducing signal strength, multiplexers/de-multiplexers for combining or separating different wavelengths, and optical amplifiers for boosting signal power. These components are essential for building advanced optical communication systems and are in high demand across various industries including telecommunications, healthcare, and aerospace.</p></p>
