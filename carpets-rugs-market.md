<p><p>Carpets & rugs market types include tufted, woven, needle-punched, knotted, and others. Tufted carpets are made by inserting yarn into a backing material. Woven carpets are created by interweaving yarn to form the carpet. Needle-punched carpets are made by pushing fibers through a backing material. Knotted carpets involve tying pile yarn around base material. Other types may include flatweave, shag, and hooked rugs. Each type offers unique characteristics in terms of texture, durability, and design.</p></p>
