<p><strong>The Antenna Surge Protector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Switch Type</li><li>Straight-through Type</li><li>Composite Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Antenna surge protectors come in three main types: Switch Type, Straight-through Type, and Composite Type. Switch Type surge protectors are designed with a switch that can be toggled off in case of a power surge. Straight-through Type surge protectors provide continuous protection without any interruption in the signal flow. Composite Type surge protectors offer a combination of features from both Switch Type and Straight-through Type, providing both surge protection and signal continuity. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
