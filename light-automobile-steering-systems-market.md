<p><p>Light automobile steering systems include hydraulic power steering, electronic power steering, and electro-hydraulic power steering. Hydraulic power steering uses hydraulic pressure to assist turning the steering wheel, electronic power steering uses an electric motor, and sensors to provide steering assistance, while electro-hydraulic power steering combines both hydraulic pressure and electric power to assist steering. These different types of steering systems offer varying levels of assistance and control for the driver, catering to different preferences and driving conditions.</p></p>
