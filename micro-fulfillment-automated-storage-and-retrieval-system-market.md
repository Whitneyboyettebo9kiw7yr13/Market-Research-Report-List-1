<p><p>Micro-Fulfillment Automated Storage and Retrieval System market can be classified into two main types - Single-mast and Multi-mast. Single-mast systems consist of a single vertical mast that moves horizontally to pick up items from storage and deliver them to the picker. On the other hand, Multi-mast systems have multiple vertical masts operating simultaneously to increase efficiency and speed of the picking process. Both types of systems offer automated storage and retrieval solutions for businesses looking to streamline their operations and improve productivity.</p></p>
