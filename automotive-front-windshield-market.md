<p><p>Automotive front windshields are available in three main types: Class A Laminated Glass, Class B Laminated Glass, and Tempered Glass. Class A Laminated Glass offers the highest level of safety and protection, while Class B Laminated Glass provides a more budget-friendly option with slightly less protection. Tempered Glass is a strong and durable option that shatters into small, blunt pieces upon impact. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market, offering a variety of options for consumers.</p></p>
