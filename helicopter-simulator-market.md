<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Helicopter Simulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FFS and FTD</li><li>ATD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Helicopter simulators are categorized into different market types based on their level of realism and capabilities. Full Flight Simulators (FFS) are the most advanced type, providing full motion, visuals, and control feedback to closely replicate real flight conditions. Flight Training Devices (FTD) offer less realism compared to FFS but are still effective training tools. Advanced Training Devices (ATD) are the most basic type, focusing on specific training objectives without full motion or complex visuals. Each market type caters to different training needs and budgets in the helicopter simulation industry.</p></p>
