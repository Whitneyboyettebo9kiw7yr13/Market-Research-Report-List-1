<p><p>The Automotive Pressure Switch market includes various types such as Dual Function Pressure Switch, HPCO, Fan, and Compressor Cycling switches. The Dual Function Pressure Switch is used to monitor both high and low-pressure levels in automotive systems. HPCO switches protect against high-pressure levels, while Fan switches control the operation of cooling fans. Compressor Cycling switches regulate the cycling of a compressor to maintain optimal pressure levels. Each type serves a different function in ensuring the proper operation and safety of automotive systems.</p></p>
