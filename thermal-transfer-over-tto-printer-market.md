<p><p>Thermal Transfer Over (TTO) Printer market is divided into two main types: Single Head and Multi Head printers. Single Head printers are equipped with one print head, suitable for applications requiring one-line printing. Multi Head printers, on the other hand, have multiple print heads, allowing for simultaneous printing of multiple lines on flexible packaging materials. Single Head printers are typically more affordable and simpler to operate, while Multi Head printers offer higher throughput and efficiency for more complex printing requirements.</p></p>
