<p><p>Lithium battery protection chips are essential components in ensuring the safe and efficient operation of lithium batteries. The market types for these chips include OV (Overvoltage Protection), which prevents the battery from being overcharged; OT (Overtemperature Protection), which safeguards against overheating; and OCD (Overcurrent Protection), which helps regulate the current flow to prevent damage to the battery. These market types cater to different aspects of battery safety, making them crucial in various applications such as mobile devices, electric vehicles, and renewable energy storage systems.</p></p>
