<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Print Servers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Print Server</li><li>External Print Server</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Internal print servers are built directly into the printer hardware, providing built-in network connectivity for easy printing from multiple devices. External print servers, on the other hand, are separate devices that connect to printers through a USB or Ethernet port, enabling network printing capabilities for non-networked printers. While internal print servers offer a more seamless integration, external print servers allow for retroactive networking of older printers, making them a cost-effective option for businesses looking to upgrade their printing capabilities.</p></p>
