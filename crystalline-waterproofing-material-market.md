<p><p>Crystalline waterproofing material market includes two main types: Crystalline waterproofing coating and Crystalline waterproofing admixtures. Crystalline waterproofing coatings are applied as a surface treatment to provide a barrier against water penetration. On the other hand, Crystalline waterproofing admixtures are added to concrete mixtures to improve the overall concrete structure's waterproofing properties. Both types of products are designed to enhance the durability and longevity of the structures by preventing water damage.</p></p>
