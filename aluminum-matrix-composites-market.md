These particles enhance the strength, stiffness, and wear resistance of the material. On the other hand, Fiber Enhanced Type uses fibers like carbon or boron to reinforce the matrix material, providing higher strength, stiffness, and thermal conductivity. Both types of composites are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and defense for applications requiring lightweight and high-performance materials.</p></p>
