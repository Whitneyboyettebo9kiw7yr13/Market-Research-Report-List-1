<p><strong>The Fine Bubble Membrane Diffuser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Disc Diffuser</li><li>Tube Diffuser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fine Bubble Membrane Diffuser Market Types consist of Disc Diffuser and Tube Diffuser. </p><p>Disc diffusers are flat discs with fine pores that release bubbles uniformly across their surface, making them ideal for high efficiency oxygen transfer in wastewater treatment plants. </p><p>Tube diffusers, on the other hand, are cylindrical membranes with perforations that release fine bubbles vertically, creating efficient mixing and aeration in tanks and ponds. Both types play a crucial role in improving oxygen transfer efficiency and overall wastewater treatment processes.</p></p>
