<p><p>Automotive active safety sensors are electronic devices that are used to detect and prevent potential accidents in vehicles. The market is driven by various factors such as strict government regulations regarding vehicle safety, the rise in consumer awareness about road safety, and the growing adoption of autonomous vehicles.
