<p><p>Wood fuel pellets come in two main types - white pellets and black pellets. White pellets are made from pure wood sawdust and have a lighter coloration. They are commonly used in residential heating applications due to their clean burn and high energy output. On the other hand, black pellets are produced through torrefaction, a process that significantly increases their energy density. Black pellets are preferred for industrial use as they are more efficient and provide a higher heat output compared to white pellets.</p></p>
