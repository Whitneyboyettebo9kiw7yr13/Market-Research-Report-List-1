<p><ul><li>Amino Acid</li><li>Hydrolyzed Protein</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Protein shampoos are categorized into two main types: amino acid-based and hydrolyzed protein-based. Amino acid protein shampoos contain essential amino acids that help nourish and strengthen the hair. On the other hand, hydrolyzed protein shampoos are formulated with proteins that have been broken down into smaller molecules for better absorption and effectiveness. Both types of protein shampoos are popular in the market for their ability to improve hair health, increase strength, and provide nourishment to damaged hair.</p></p>
