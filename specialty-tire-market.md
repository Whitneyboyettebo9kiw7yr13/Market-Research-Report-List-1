<p><strong>The Specialty Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tires</li><li>Bias (Crossply) Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Specialty tire market types include radial tires and bias (crossply) tires. Radial tires are constructed with cords laid at a 90-degree angle to the direction of travel, providing better traction and performance on various terrains. Bias tires, on the other hand, are constructed with cords laid diagonally across the tire, offering a smoother ride and greater stability at higher speeds. Both types cater to specific needs and preferences, with radial tires being favored for their durability and longevity, while bias tires are known for their affordability and versatility.</p></p>
