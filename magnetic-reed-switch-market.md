<p><p>Magnetic reed switches are available in three main types: Form A, Form B, and Form C. Form A switches are normally open and close their contacts when a magnetic field is detected. Form B switches are normally closed and open their contacts in the presence of a magnetic field. Form C switches have one normally closed and one normally open contact, allowing them to perform both functions simultaneously. Each type of switch is suited for different applications based on the desired behavior of the contacts.</p></p>
