<p><p>The Fiberglass Wallpapers market is divided into two main types: Non-Woven Fabric and Woven Fabric. Non-woven fabric wallpapers are made by bonding fibers together using heat or chemicals, creating a sheet-like material. These wallpapers are lightweight, easy to install, and provide good sound insulation. On the other hand, woven fabric wallpapers are made by weaving fibers into a fabric, resulting in a more textured and durable product. They offer a wide range of patterns and designs and are often chosen for their aesthetic appeal and longevity.</p></p>
