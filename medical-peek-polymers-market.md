<p><p>Medical PEEK Polymers are available in two main forms in the market - Powder and Particle. On the other hand, Particle form consists of small granules of PEEK polymers that are used in applications where a higher level of strength and durability is required. Both forms of Medical PEEK Polymers offer unique advantages in the healthcare industry for their biocompatibility, resistance to chemicals, and excellent mechanical properties.</p></p>
