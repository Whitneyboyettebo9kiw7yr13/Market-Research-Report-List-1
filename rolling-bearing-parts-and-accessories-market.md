<p><ul><li>Centripetal Type</li><li>Scroll Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rolling bearing parts and accessories market types include centripetal type and scroll type. Centripetal type bearings use balls or rollers to rotate around a central axis, allowing for smooth rotation in a single direction. Scroll type bearings, on the other hand, use interlocking spirals that generate both linear and rotational motion. Both types are essential components in various machinery and equipment, providing support and reducing friction for moving parts. These market types cater to different industries and applications that require reliable and efficient bearing solutions.</p></p>
