<p><ul><li>Passive Suspension System</li><li>Semi-Active Suspension System</li><li>Active Suspension System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial vehicle suspension system market is divided into three main types - Passive Suspension System, Semi-Active Suspension System, and Active Suspension System. Passive suspension systems are the most basic and traditional type, providing a fixed level of damping. Semi-active suspension systems offer variable damping for improved comfort and handling, while active suspension systems use sensors and actuators to actively adjust damping levels in real time for optimal performance. Each type offers a different level of control and customization for commercial vehicles.</p></p>
