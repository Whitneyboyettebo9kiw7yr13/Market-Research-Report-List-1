<p><p>Flame Retardant Masterbatch for Engineering Plastics comes in two main types: Halogens Flame Retardant Masterbatch and Halogen Free Flame Retardant Masterbatch. </p><p>Halogens Flame Retardant Masterbatch contains chemicals like bromine or chlorine, which help in preventing the spread of fire by releasing flame-inhibiting gases. On the other hand, Halogen Free Flame Retardant Masterbatch consists of non-halogenated compounds like phosphorus or nitrogen to achieve flame retardancy without the use of harmful halogen elements. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the fire safety of engineering plastics.</p></p>
