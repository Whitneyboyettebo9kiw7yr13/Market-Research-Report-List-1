<p><p>Smartphone OLED Display market includes two main types: Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode (AMOLED) Display and Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED) Display. AMOLED displays offer higher resolution, faster response times, and better color reproduction due to each pixel having its own transistor. This makes them more popular in high-end smartphones. PMOLED displays are simpler and cheaper to produce, making them suitable for simpler devices like wearable technology. Both types offer vibrant colors, deep blacks, and energy efficiency compared to traditional LCD displays.</p></p>
