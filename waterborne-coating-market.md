<p><p>Waterborne coating is a type of coating that uses water as a solvent to disperse the resin used to make the coating. This type of coating is becoming increasingly popular due to its eco-friendly nature, as it emits lower levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) compared to traditional solvent-based coatings. Waterborne coatings are widely used in industries such as construction, automotive, aerospace, and furniture.</p><p>The waterborne coating market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.3% during the forecast period. The growth of the market can be attributed to increasing awareness about environmental issues and regulations mandating the use of eco-friendly coatings. Additionally, the rising demand for waterborne coatings in various end-use industries due to their superior properties such as low toxicity, high durability, and excellent corrosion resistance is driving the market growth.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the waterborne coating market include the development of innovative formulations with improved performance characteristics, increasing focus on research and development activities to enhance the sustainability of coatings, and the adoption of waterborne coatings in new applications such as packaging and marine coatings. Overall, the waterborne coating market is expected to continue to expand in the coming years due to the growing preference for eco-friendly and sustainable coating solutions.</p></p>
