Manual focus lenses require the photographer to manually adjust the focus by turning a ring on the lens, providing more control over the final image. Autofocus lenses, on the other hand, use a motorized system to automatically adjust the focus based on the subject in the frame. Both types of lenses cater to different photography styles and preferences, offering varied options for photographers in the market.</p></p>
