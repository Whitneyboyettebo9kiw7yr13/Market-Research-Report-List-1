<p><ul><li>Glass Fiber</li><li>Mineral Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber acoustical panels are divided into two main types: glass fiber and mineral fiber. Glass fiber acoustical panels are made from tiny fibers of glass woven into a matting material, providing excellent sound absorption properties. On the other hand, mineral fiber acoustical panels are made from natural or synthetic minerals and binders, offering a more eco-friendly option. Both types of panels are commonly used in the construction industry for their ability to improve sound quality in various spaces such as offices, schools, and restaurants.</p></p>
