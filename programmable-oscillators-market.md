<p><strong>The Programmable Oscillators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through Hole Programmable Oscillators</li><li>Surface Mount Programmable Oscillators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Programmable oscillators can be categorized into two main types: Through Hole Programmable Oscillators and Surface Mount Programmable Oscillators. Through Hole Programmable Oscillators are mounted on a circuit board using leads that go through pre-drilled holes, providing a more secure mechanical connection. On the other hand, Surface Mount Programmable Oscillators are mounted directly onto the surface of the circuit board, which can save space and reduce overall assembly costs. Both types offer programmable frequency options for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
