<p><strong>The 1, 4 Butanediol Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reppe process</li><li>Davy process</li><li>Butadiene process</li><li>Propylene oxide process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 1,4 butanediol market offers various types of production processes. The Reppe process involves reacting acetylene with formaldehyde to yield 1,4 butanediol. The Davy process uses a two-step reaction involving propylene oxide and formaldehyde to produce the compound. The Butadiene process employs butadiene as the main raw material, which is oxidized and then reacted with formaldehyde to form 1,4 butanediol. Lastly, the Propylene oxide process utilizes propylene oxide as a starting material, which undergoes hydrogenation to obtain 1,4 butanediol. These different processes cater to diverse market demands.</p></p>
