<p><p>Vehicle lenses market can be divided into two main types - external lenses and interior lenses. These lenses are designed to withstand harsh weather conditions and provide optimal visibility for drivers. On the other hand, interior lenses refer to the lenses used on the interior of the vehicle, such as instrument cluster lenses and rearview mirror lenses. These lenses are designed to provide clear and accurate information to the driver while ensuring safety on the road.</p></p>
