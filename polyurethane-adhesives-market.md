<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Polyurethane Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-borne</li><li>Reactive</li><li>Dispersion</li><li>Hot-melt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane adhesives are available in various types to suit different application needs. Solvent-borne adhesives are dissolved in solvents for easy application and drying. Reactive adhesives contain curing agents that react chemically to form strong bonds. Dispersion adhesives are water-based, offering environmental benefits and easy cleanup. Hot-melt adhesives are applied in a molten state and solidify upon cooling, providing a quick bonding solution. Each type of polyurethane adhesive offers unique benefits and is used in different industries based on specific requirements.</p></p>
