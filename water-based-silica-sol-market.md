<p><p>Water-based silica sol markets can be classified into three main types: alkaline, acidic, and others. Alkaline silica sols have a pH level above 7, making them ideal for applications where a basic environment is desired. Acidic silica sols, on the other hand, have a pH level below 7, making them suitable for environments that require acidity. The "others" category encompasses silica sols with unique properties or pH levels that do not fit into the alkaline or acidic categories. Each type of silica sol offers different benefits and is used in various industries based on their specific properties.</p></p>
