<p><strong>The Synthetic Butadiene Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxidative Dehydrogenation</li><li>Extractive Distillation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic butadiene market can be categorized into two types: oxidative dehydrogenation and extractive distillation. Oxidative dehydrogenation involves the conversion of butane into butadiene using a catalyst and oxygen. This process is energy-intensive but cost-effective. On the other hand, extractive distillation involves the separation of butadiene from its impurities using a solvent. This method offers high purity but is relatively expensive. These two methods are commonly used in the synthetic butadiene market for the production of rubber, plastics, and other industrial products.</p></p>
