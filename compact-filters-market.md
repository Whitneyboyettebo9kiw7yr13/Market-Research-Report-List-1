ISO ePM1 filters are designed to capture the smallest particles, ISO ePM2.5 filters capture particles of intermediate size, and ISO ePM10 filters capture larger particles. These filters are used in various industries and applications where air quality and cleanliness are crucial, such as in hospitals, manufacturing plants, and commercial buildings.</p></p>
