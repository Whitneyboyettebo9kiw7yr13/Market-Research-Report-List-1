<p><p>Hard seal butterfly valves are available in two market types: flangedless and flanged. Flangedless butterfly valves do not have the traditional flange attachment and instead use a lug or wafer design for installation. These valves are typically more compact and require less space for installation. On the other hand, flanged butterfly valves have a flange attachment for easy connection to pipelines. They are known for their durability and ability to withstand high pressure and temperature conditions. Both types offer reliable and precise control in various industrial applications.</p></p>
