<p><p>Automatic timing devices market consists of various types such as oscillators, clock buffers, clock generators, jitter attenuators, and resonators. Oscillators provide precise frequency reference for electronic circuits. Clock buffers help in distributing clock signals across a system. Clock generators create clock signals for digital circuits. Jitter attenuators reduce timing variations in signals. Resonators are used to provide stable frequency references. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring proper timing and synchronization in electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
