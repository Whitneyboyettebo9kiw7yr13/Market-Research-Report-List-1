<p><ul><li>Water Bottle Grade</li><li>Hot Filling Grade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bottle grade polyester chips are mainly categorized into two types: water bottle grade and hot filling grade. Water bottle grade chips are designed to meet the specific requirements of producing water bottles, ensuring they have excellent clarity, good mechanical properties, and resistance to chemicals. On the other hand, hot filling grade chips are specifically formulated for bottles used in hot filling applications, such as juices and teas. These chips have enhanced heat resistance to withstand the high temperatures involved in the filling process.</p></p>
