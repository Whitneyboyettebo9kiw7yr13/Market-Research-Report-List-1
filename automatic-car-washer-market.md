<p><p>The automatic car washer market can be divided into two main types: Gantry Car Wash and Conveyor Tunnel System. Gantry car wash systems involve an overhead structure that moves back and forth over the vehicle as it is washed. These systems are typically located at gas stations and car wash facilities. Conveyor tunnel systems, on the other hand, use a conveyor belt to move vehicles through a series of cleaning stages. These systems are more commonly found at standalone car wash facilities or large-scale dealership operations.</p></p>
