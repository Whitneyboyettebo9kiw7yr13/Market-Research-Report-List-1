<p><p>Rotary torque sensors are devices used to measure the torque applied to a rotating system. These sensors come in two main types - with encoder and without encoder. The with encoder type includes additional encoder technology to provide precise angular measurement along with torque data. On the other hand, without encoder sensors only measure torque without providing information on the rotation angle. Both types are used in various industrial applications such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing for monitoring and controlling torque levels.</p></p>
