<p><p>The sedan vehicle thermostat market consists of two main types: Insert Thermostats and Housing Thermostats. Insert Thermostats are smaller in size and are inserted directly into the engine block. They are often more affordable and easier to replace. Housing Thermostats, on the other hand, include both the thermostat and the housing unit. They are usually larger and more complex, but offer better durability and longevity. Both types play a crucial role in regulating the engine temperature and ensuring optimal performance of the vehicle.</p></p>
