<p><p>The windshield wipers market is divided into two main types: bone wipers and boneless wipers. Bone wipers are traditional wipers that have a metal frame resembling a bone structure, while boneless wipers do not have a metal frame and instead rely on a more flexible design to move across the windshield. Bone wipers are known for their durability and strength, while boneless wipers are praised for their sleek design and smoother operation. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and needs in the automotive industry.</p></p>
