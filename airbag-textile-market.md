<p><strong>The Airbag Textile Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Airbag Fabric</li><li>OPW Airbag Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Airbag textile market is segmented into two main types: flat airbag fabric and OPW airbag fabric. Flat airbag fabric is made using traditional weaving techniques, providing a simple and cost-effective solution for airbag production. On the other hand, OPW (one-piece woven) airbag fabric is produced using advanced weaving technology, resulting in a seamless and high-performance material. Both types serve the same purpose of providing strength and protection in automotive airbag systems, but OPW fabric offers superior quality and durability compared to flat fabric.</p></p>
