<p><p>Anchor bolts are essential components in construction projects, available in two main types: fixed anchor bolts and movable anchor bolts. Fixed anchor bolts are designed to securely attach structures to concrete or masonry, providing stability and support. On the other hand, movable anchor bolts are adjustable and can be repositioned as needed during construction. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and durability of structures, with fixed anchor bolts offering permanent anchoring solutions and movable anchor bolts providing flexibility in construction projects.</p></p>
