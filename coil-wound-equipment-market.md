<p><p>Coil wound equipment market includes various types such as sensors, bobbins, electromagnetic coils, solenoids, and lightning coils. Sensors are devices that detect and respond to electrical or optical signals. Bobbins are spools on which insulated wires are wound. Electromagnetic coils generate magnetic fields when an electric current passes through them. Solenoids are electromechanical devices that convert electrical energy into linear motion. Lightning coils are designed to protect buildings from lightning strikes by dissipating the electrical charge safely into the ground.</p></p>
