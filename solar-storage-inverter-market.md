<p><strong>The Solar Storage Inverter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>String Inverter</li><li>Central Inverter</li><li>Microinverters</li><li>Hybrid Inverters</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>String inverters are typically used in residential and small commercial solar PV systems, where multiple solar panels are connected in series. Central inverters are commonly used in large-scale solar installations, converting the DC power generated by the solar panels into AC power for the grid. Microinverters are installed on each individual solar panel, maximizing energy production and efficiency. Hybrid inverters combine solar power with battery storage, allowing for greater energy independence. Other types of inverters include power optimizers and charge controllers.</p></p>
