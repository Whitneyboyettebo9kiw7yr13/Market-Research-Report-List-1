<p><ul><li>Twisted Pair(Copper)</li><li>Coax(Copper)</li><li>Optic Cables(Fibre)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Signal & Data Cables market consists of three main types: Twisted Pair (Copper), Coax (Copper), and Optic Cables (Fibre). Twisted Pair cables use copper wires twisted together to reduce interference. Coaxial cables have a copper core surrounded by insulating material and a second conducting layer. Optic cables use fibre optics for data transmission through light signals. Each type has its own strengths and weaknesses, catering to different needs in terms of speed, distance, and interference resistance in various industries.</p></p>
